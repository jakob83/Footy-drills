This webpage is a tool for building your own individual football training sessions. If you are a baller on the grind or just want to improve some aspects of your game, this is the perfect tool to use. It lets you build and organize your sessions. You can also include videos from youtube or selfmade to your drills. This tool will also let you deside how many drills you'd like to do in one session. This can also help you to find the motivation required to go on the pitch and train individualy. It isn't easy to go and train by yourself, but i know you are going to make it!
